"SISTERS" BAKERY

Many families that immigrated to the United States in the 1800s and early 1900s and found their way in America by opening small independent bakeries. Decades later many of those bakeries are still thriving, often run by new generations of the original bakers’ families. Bakeries are an essential part of communities across the country – these are some of the oldest that are still going strong, often more than 100 years after they first opened. Be sure to check individual websites for the latest opening updates before you visit.
